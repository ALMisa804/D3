BREAKING: Senate voting on new health care system

The Senate is now set to vote on the Medicare For All bill which was passed by the House of Representatives in the first multi-party coalition vote pushed by Libertarian members of the House. Nationwide economic protests along with studies confirming the strong correlation between poverty and the lack of access to healthcare have finally pushed Congress to act on healthcare reform. A close vote is expected.
